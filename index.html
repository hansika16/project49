Interesting Facts About Dogs

  • The tallest dog in the world ever was a Great Dane named Zeus! Measured at 44 inches tall on October 4th 2011, he’s the current Guinness World Record holder.
  • A Greyhound could beat a Cheetah in a long distance race
  • The African Hunting dog is the most successful hunter in the world

Interesting Facts About Turtles

  • The oldest known sea turtle fossil is at least 120 million years old, making sea turtles some of the oldest creatures on the planet.
  • Female marine turtles return to the same nesting grounds where they were born when it’s time for them to lay their own eggs.
  • The leatherback turtle is the largest of all living turtles and can grow to weigh as much as 900 kg.

Interesting Facts About Pikachu

  • It evolves from Pichu when levelled up with high happiness. Pikachu evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunderstone.
  • “Pika” is the Japanese word for the noise of electricity crackling. “Chu” is how the Japanese describe the sound a mouse makes. That’s how the name “Pikachu” was created.
